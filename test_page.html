DrugGuard extension will automatically scan all text blocks below. Drug content will be  blurred (blocked)  or marked with a  yellow outline (warned). Safe text stays unchanged.
🔴 High Risk — Expect: BLURRED / BLOCKED
Direct trafficking text

Got powder available for pickup, DM for prices. Fresh batch just arrived.

Supply chain language

Moving pills across the border this weekend. Wholesale goods available, bulk discounts apply.

Coded slang

Need crystal ASAP, anyone near downtown can deliver? My plug just re-upped, quality stash.

Emoji-coded (algospeak)

Got that 🍃🍃🍃 ready, DM me for prices. 🔌 best ❄️ in town, hit me up. Fresh 💊💊 just landed, first come first served.

🟢 Safe Content — Expect: NO CHANGE
Everyday conversation

Just finished reading a great textbook about machine learning. Really enjoying the course.

Shopping / marketplace

Selling my old laptop on the marketplace, barely used. Comes with original charger and box.

Social plans

Going to the park this weekend with family. The weather in the bay area has been beautiful lately.

Study / academic

Looking for study partners for the math exam next week. Anyone want to join a coding study group?

🟡 Borderline — Expect: WARNING (yellow outline)
Ambiguous delivery language

Fast delivery guaranteed to your doorstep. Discreet packaging available on request.

Vague product listing

Looking for a connect in the area. Need a reliable supplier with good product quality.

📰 News / Educational — Expect: SAFE
Journalism context

Police seized a large shipment of illegal substances at the border checkpoint. Three suspects were arrested.

Academic / research

The study analyzed social media platforms for patterns of drug-related activity using NLP techniques.
